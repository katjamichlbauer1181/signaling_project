  1. A target symbol is shown at the top of each trial.
  2. Below it is a grid of 64 symbols. Some of them match the target; others do not.
  3. Click every cell that contains the target symbol. Click again to deselect a cell.
  4. When you are done, click Confirm Selection to submit and move to the next trial.
  5. The number of matching symbols varies from trial to trial — sometimes there is only one; sometimes there are several.
No feedback is given after each trial — the next trial starts immediately. Just do your best and work at a steady pace.
